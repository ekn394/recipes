Porcupines A La Sheldon

1 pound ground beef 
1 minced onion.
1 large egg.
1 cup instant rice to start (add more by the quarter cup, until it holds it meatball shape)
Salt and pepper to taste.
In a covered baking dish, place meatballs in a single layer(if possible)
Add water/beef broth
Bake, covered on 350 for 45 minutes. 
Remove from oven, remove meatballs,
Use remaining liquid in pan to make gravy of your choice.
Re-add meatballs to gravy, stir to coat, serve.

Seasonings for meatballs (prior to cooking)
- garlic
- dill weed
- basil
