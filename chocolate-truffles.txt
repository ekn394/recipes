Chocolate Truffles - From Mom’s recipe cards

Centres
½ lb butter
1 can of condensed milk
3 lbs icing sugar
Flavor & colouring of choice
Coating
14tsp icing sugar (reduced by 10)
2 ½ wax (paraffin) (reduced by 2)
6 squares of unsweetened chocolate

Can add cherries and/or nuts can be added to the centre.

Once filling is coated with chocolate/paraffin mixture, chill to set.
